Frequently Asked Questions

How do I unlock all sounds?

To unlock all sounds, you can make a one-time in-app purchase of $5. This will give you access to the full range of sounds available in Chord Colors.

Is my purchase transferable to other devices?

Yes, your in-app purchase is tied to your App Store account. You can restore your purchase on any device where you're signed in with the same account.

How do I report a bug?

If you encounter any bugs or issues, please email us at chordcolors@gmail.com. Include as much detail as possible, including your device model, iOS version, and clear instructions on how to reproduce the issue. Screenshots or videos are extremely helpful to triage and debug issues.
