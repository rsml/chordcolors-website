Privacy Policy

Introduction

At Chord Colors, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and use for our free app, Chord Colors.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data from our users. Chord Colors is designed to function without the need for user data collection.

In-App Purchases

Chord Colors offers an optional in-app purchase of $5 to unlock all sounds. This transaction is processed through the app store platform (e.g., Apple App Store) and we do not receive or store any payment information.

Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party services that collect data on our behalf.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at chordcolors@gmail.com.
