Frequently Asked Questions

Is Chord Colors really free?

Absolutely! Chord Colors is free to download and use. There is a one time in-app purchase to unlock some of the advanced features, but the core functionality is available at no cost.

Do I need to know music theory to use Chord Colors?

Not at all! Chord Colors is designed to be intuitive for beginners while still offering depth for advanced musicians. The unique visual approach makes learning music theory more fun and accessible.

Can I use Chord Colors offline?

Yes, once you've downloaded the app, you can use all of Chord Colors' features offline. Perfect for when inspiration strikes, no matter where you are!
